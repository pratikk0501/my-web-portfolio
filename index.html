A Dockerized and authentication-protected Todo App made using NodeJS, ExpressJS, Node-Cron, Nodemailer, bcrypt, JWT authentication, Prisma, and PostgreSQL. The app allows users to:

  1. Register: Create a new account.
  2. Login/Logout: Authenticate and receive a JWT token; logout clears the token.
  3. Manage Todos: Perform auth-protected CRUD operations on their own todo tasks after logging in.
  4. Email Reminders: Receive email reminders for todo completion 30 mins before due time.
